Commands
==========================
s - Save

l - Load

z - undo

y - redo

delete - reset block img

0 to 9 (*) - select between blocks imgs

alt left - last roll of img(**)

alt right - next roll of img(**) 

f - fill Forward with block img

b - fill Backward with block img

u - fill Up with block img

d - fill Down with block img

a - fill all with block img

insert - clear all

Mouse Click - select block


NOTES:
==============
(*) This is called as a roll of img (select between  0 and 9 numbers) with a total of 3 rolls of img (3 * [0,9] = 30 img supported)
(**) What is a "roll of img"? Read (*) up here ^
